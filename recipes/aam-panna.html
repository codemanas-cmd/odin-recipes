Aam panna (Boiled Mango juice)

Ingredients:-

  • 2 Green raw Mangoes
  • 3 tbsp Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Cumin powder
  • 2 tbsp Black salt
  • Mint leaves
  • Ice cubes

steps:-

  1. Place the mangoes along with water in a sauce-pan and Boil them. Simmer for 10 minutes until it gets soft from inside.
  2. Let the mangoes cool and then peel them off with the help of a spoon.
  3. Grind the pulp with appropriate amount of water to make a thick paste of it.
  4. Now put the paste in a pan and add sugar. Let it cook on the flame till the sugar dissolves completely.
  5. Keep stirring it continuously or else it will burn.
  6. After the sugar has dissolved completely, remove the pan from fire to add cumin powder, black salt and salt to the mixture.
  7. To make the drink: Add 1-2 tbsp of the mango mixture to a tall glass of chilled water. Mix well.
  8. Garnish with mint leaves and serve.
