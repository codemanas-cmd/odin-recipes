Samosa

Ingredients:-

  • 1 cup all purpose flour (Maida)
  • 1/8 cup oil
  • 4 boiled and mashed potaotes
  • 1/2 cup green Peas
  • Spices -
    • Garam masala
    • Dry mango powder (Amchur)
    • Red chilli powder
    • Pepper and jeera (cumin) powder

Steps:-

  1. Mix flour, salt, water, oil and red chilli powder to make a dough.
  2. It should not be very soft, leave aside for 15 minutes.
  3. Heat two tsp oil and add mashed potatoes and peas, cover on low flame for 5 minutes
  4. Add garam masala, pepper, amchur and jeera powder, mix well and again cover for 5-8 minutes.
  5. Keep aside to cool.
  6. Roll small balls of dough like chapati.
  7. Cut each in two parts (like semi-circle), then take one semi circle and fold it in the shape of a cone. Use water while doing so.
  8. Stuff the mixture of potatoes, peas and spices in that cone and seal it by taking a drop of water on your finger.
  9. Heat oil and fry.
  10. Serve hot with tamarind chutney or green chutney.
